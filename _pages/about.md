<span class="dm-mark">Science</span>: an [NLP](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~ehn/11-611/) course pulled me into shared tasks ([ArabicNLP '24](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.01360), [CLEF '24](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.10252)). One thing led to another. A [QNRF](http://blogs.qu.edu.qa/cse/tag/qnrf/)-funded [CMUQ](https://www.qatar.cmu.edu/news/carnegie-mellon-receives-grants-for-ai-research-into-key-areas-for-qatar/) &times; [QU](http://qufaculty.qu.edu.qa/telsayed/current_research_projects/) collaboration on automated essay scoring for Arabic, a new dataset ([EACL '26](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24235)).

<span class="dm-mark">Engineering</span>: a full-stack app for the Genomics Data Science Core at [Sidra Medicine](https://www.sidra.org/). Automated data extraction; interactive access to genomic data and [NGS](https://www.illumina.com/science/technology/next-generation-sequencing.html) metadata for researchers and clinicians ([SARF '26](/assets/pdf/SARF-2026-Poster.pdf)). Building responsible software on top of fragmented, high-stakes data (especially when the outputs feed straight into precision medicine) is what makes it ... 'fun'.

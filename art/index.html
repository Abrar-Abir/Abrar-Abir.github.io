Eman | أنصار

A dual-language ambigram — EMAN in English and أنصار (Ansar) in Arabic (after rotation & mirroring). Bold, monochrome, minimal: with a timeless look. ‘Eman’ reads immediately; ‘أنصار’ is exclusive to the attentive viewer. A tribute to Eman Ansar - learned a lot from collaboration

GiSelle | reiS

Ongoing work on fusion typography — merging ‘Giselle’ and ‘Reis’ into a single connected glyph. The nontrivial part is the asymmetric length: the ‘elle’ triangle still needs work for contrast, and the first ‘e’ isn’t clean enough yet. A tribute to Professor Giselle Reis - Sherpa of my Undergrad journey

Vectr.com | 2018

أبرار | Abir

Draft sketch of a duoingual fusion typography — ‘Abir’ in Latin script and ‘أبرار’ (Abrar) in Arabic, visible in the same glyph. Harder than a monolingual version: the two scripts run in opposite directions, so making both readings feel natural simultaneously is an unsolved problem in this sketch.

Hexagonal Game of Life

Python | 2024

Hexagonal Game of Life

A hexagonal variant of Conway’s Game of Life built with Pygame for 21-150: Mathematics & the Arts. Each cell has six neighbors instead of eight, producing organic growth patterns under modified survival rules. The script supports both random and deterministic initialization.

Python | 2024

Julia Set

Python | 2024

Julia Set

A Julia Set fractal rendered in Python (NumPy + Matplotlib) for 21-150: Mathematics & the Arts. The script iterates z = z² + c over a 6400 × 6400 complex-plane grid, counting escape times to produce the image. Changing the constant c = −0.8 + 0.156i yields entirely different fractal forms.

Python | 2024

GTI Hoodie

Apple Notes | 2024

GTI Hoodie

Hoodie design for Course Assistants in 15-251 (Great Theoretical Ideas in Computer Science) — one of the hardest courses at CMU, and one of the most formative. The design (inspired from the design at the official course website for Pittsburgh campus) tries to capture what it feels like to be inside it: a whiteboard overflowing with ideas that are individually hard and collectively overwhelming. The caption is the course’s implicit promise — the material doesn’t get easier, but you’re not supposed to face it alone. That’s what CAs are for. Credit to Rama Sulaiman for lending the iPad.

Apple Notes | 2024
